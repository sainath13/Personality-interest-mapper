How it Works?

Twitter -> Personality Insights -> Relation


Action Flow of the Application


Twitter

User enters a Twitter handle. The profile must be Public. There should be at least 100 different words in english in Tweets data. The more the Tweets, the more the accuracy of OCEAN model result by Personality insights

Watson Personality insights

The IBM Watson Personality Insights service uses linguistic analytics to infer cognitive and social characteristics, including Big Five, Values, and Needs, from tweets data.

Relation

To predict the relation between personality and entertainment domains we used a paper named "Relating Personality Types with User Preferences in Multiple Entertainment Domains" by Iván Cantador,Ignacio Fernández-Tobías,Alejandro Bellogín.You can look at the paper here.CEUR Workshop Proceedings (CEUR-WS.org) is a free open-access publication service of Sun SITE Central Europe operated under the umbrella of RWTH Aachen University.
